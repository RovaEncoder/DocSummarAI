Introduction to Kubernetes:
Kubernetes is an open-source container orchestration platform designed to automate deployment, scaling, and management of applications. 
It allows developers to deploy containerized applications efficiently across clusters, ensuring high availability and scalability. 
Kubernetes provides features such as load balancing, service discovery, and self-healing, making it a powerful tool for managing complex infrastructures.
